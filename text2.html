Ooty (listen (help·info)), officially known as Udhagamandalam (also known as Ootacamund (listen (help·info)); abbreviated as Udhagai), is a town[4] and a municipality in the Nilgiris district of the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located 86 km (53 mi) north west of Coimbatore, 100 km (65 mi) north west of Tirupur,[5] 128 km (80 mi) south of Mysore and is the headquarters of the Nilgiris district. It is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills. It is popularly called the "Queen of Hill Stations". It was the summer capital of the Madras Presidency.
